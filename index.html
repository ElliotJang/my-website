About Me 나에 대해

Hello! My name is Elliot Jang, the founder of Elliot's Classes, and a current student at UC Berkeley. Since high school, I’ve been passionate about teaching and have helped elementary and middle school students overcome academic challenges. I graduated high school summa cum laude (top 5% of my class) and maintained Honor Roll status throughout all of my school years.

So far, I’ve had the opportunity to teach over 100 elementary and middle school students. During high school, I taught online coding classes to elementary students and also provided online lessons to middle school students in Tanzania. Even now, through a university organization, I continue to teach STEM classes to local elementary schools.

Currently, I offer tutoring in Math and English for elementary and middle school students. If you're in a different grade or need help with another subject, feel free to reach out! I’d be happy to connect you with other UC Berkeley students who can provide support in more advanced classes.

Elliot's Classes features passionate university tutors with strong academic backgrounds and extensive teaching experience. We provide personalized one-on-one online tutoring via Discord, along with homework and practice problems tailored to each student’s needs. Students are also encouraged to ask questions on Discord anytime, even outside of class.

Specifically, all of our tutors for high school students have a proven academic track record, including 5s on AP exams and A grades in the subject(s) they teach.
