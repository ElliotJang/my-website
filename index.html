About Me 나에 대해

Hello! I’m Elliot Jang, a student at UC Berkeley. Since high school, I’ve been passionate about teaching and helping young students succeed academically. I graduated summa cum laude (top 5%) in high school, which reflects both my strong academic foundation and dedication to education.

Over the years, I’ve had the opportunity to teach over a hundred elementary and middle school students. In high school, I led virtual coding lessons for elementary students and also worked with middle school students in Tanzania. Currently, I teach STEM lessons to local elementary students through a college organization.

I offer online tutoring through Discord, where I provide personalized one-on-one sessions. I also offer additional support outside of class, allowing students to reach out with questions anytime. If needed, I can assign homework or provide practice problems to reinforce lessons and help students achieve their goals.
